Reflection

When we started, we immediately thought of intuitive and simple designs to implement. We went with a “mobile-last” policy since we thought that would be easier with the HTML and CSS skills we had at our disposal. It dawned on us that putting pictures in a grid and then for mobile putting them on a flow would be a cool challenge. Hence the wireframes being as you can see above.

When it came to actually implementing the design, we can say it went decently for the big screens. The split grid-ish layout resulted in us having to learn a lot about how the grid works and we can say that we were very satisfied with the outcome. On the other hand implementing the design for mobile proved challenging seeing as both centering the page and the text were difficult. In the end you can still see that the footer is not entirely centered and we simply couldn’t understand why. After trying for several hours to center the footer we just gave up and decided it wasn’t a big deal.

To conclude we thought the assignment was good fun, and we learnt alot while making this website. Not everything is perfect at the moment like we explained with the footer in the mobile version of the website. Anyhow we feel like we did everything we could and we are happy with the results.
